The Eras Tour

Described by the one and only Taylor Swift as a «journey through all of my musical eras», The Eras Tour is
the sixth concert tour our beloved girl performs. This comes after not having gone through the album tours of Lover,
Folklore, Evermore and recently released, Midnights due to Covid-19. So, if you get a ticket for this concert, we vow you.
